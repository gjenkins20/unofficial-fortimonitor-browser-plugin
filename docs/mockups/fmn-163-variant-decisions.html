Section 2 - Page-selection loader

What happens when the operator arrives at the pick step with a fresh page-selection cookie (rows ticked on /report/ListServers within the last 5 minutes)?

Variant 1Button (current behavior)

No auto-anything. The operator clicks the loader explicitly. Predictable; no surprise loads.

Load instances

Drop CSV, paste a list, or load from clipboard / current page.

Clipboard: paste server IDs or names. Page selection: read your last subset ticked on /report/ListServers.

Tradeoffs

  • Zero surprise; nothing happens until the operator clicks.
  • Operator may forget the page-selection feature exists (no nudge).
  • Matches Port Scope's no-magic principle.
Variant 2Auto-load + dismiss · subtle

If a fresh page-selection cookie exists on step enter, load it and show a subtle muted banner with a Clear affordance. Loaders remain available as buttons below.

Load instances

Drop CSV, paste a list, or load from clipboard / current page.

Tradeoffs

  • Discoverable - operator who didn't know the feature exists sees the result instantly.
  • Subtle styling = minimal visual intrusion.
  • One-click clear if the operator wanted a fresh sheet.
  • Risk: operator overlooks the banner and is surprised that the textarea has content they didn't paste.
Variant 3Auto-load + banner · prominent

Auto-load + a green confirmation banner that stays until dismissed. Most discoverable; loudest UI.
