Network Quality (VoIP-adjacent) Report - mockup

FMN-166 design proposal. Two surfaces shown: the Canned Reports card injected on /report/ListReports, and the extension-served Network Quality Report itself. Real per-instance / per-metric data is from the live tenant survey (FMN-166 probes).

Surface 1 - Canned Reports card (on /report/ListReports)

Tile shows alongside FortiMonitor's 19 native report cards. The FM Toolkit ribbon marks it as extension-added, matching FMN-154's pattern.
